Seefood

Tinder. But with your food.

Seefood is an application that is meant to tackle the problem of overchoice that is prominent in our current society. Because there are so many choices presented for what to eat, we often find ourselves rather conflicted as to what to eat for each meal. Thus we might choose our go-to restaurant or simply go for fast food. Apps like Yelp or OpenTable are all restaurant oriented, so even if you find a restaurant that you like from a large list, you must then find items that you want to eat.

Recommenders try to find types of restaurants tha you might like, but the things that you like tend to be the individual items themselves. With Seefood, we aim to help users decide and discover what to eat by presenting food items one at a time. The user would then only have to choose "Yes, I'd like to eat that" or "No, let's pass." This makes the decision process so much easier. Because our app focuses on the food instead of the restaurant, we can allow the user to filter on dietary restrictions such as gluten sensitivity, Kosher, or vegan/vegetarian dish choices, for example.
